Toned Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026

Toned ("the App") is a workout planning and logging app developed by Sean. This policy explains how information is handled when you use the App.

Information we collect

Toned does not collect, transmit, or sell personal information. The App does not require an account and does not connect to our servers.

Data stored on your device

The App stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Workout sessions and logged sets
  • Your weekly training plan and exercise library
  • In-progress workout state
  • Dark or light mode preference

This data stays on your device and is not sent to us. If you uninstall the App or clear app data, this information may be deleted.

Third-party services

Toned does not use analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking SDKs.

Children’s privacy

Toned is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
